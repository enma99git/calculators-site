# Practical Calculators Weekly Monitoring Checklist

## Search Console and Indexing
- Confirm `sitemap.xml` fetch status is successful.
- Check indexed vs excluded trend for the last 7 days.
- Review newly discovered pages (`/es/` and `/us/`) for coverage status.
- Validate that intentionally `noindex` pages remain excluded.

## Monetization Readiness
- Verify trust blocks remain present on top finance/currency templates.
- Spot-check policy pages and contact page availability.
- Confirm ad slots (when enabled) do not appear before core calculator interaction.

## Traffic and Engagement (GA4)
- Review top landing calculator templates.
- Review `calculator_button_click` and `calculator_result_rendered` event counts.
- Compare engagement between core pages and pilot market pages.

## Content Quality and Pruning
- Identify low-engagement templates for consolidation candidates.
- Validate internal links from each core page to home + hub + related calculators.
- Confirm new pages include unique explanatory copy and not just formula-only content.
